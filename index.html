A crafted platform for folk art by blockchain

HiCraft

We are delivering a platform to let millions of craft lovers (masters, apprentices, customers) around the world get connected, both online and offline, to learn, make, sell, and buy unique handicrafts.

By using blockchain technology, an individual can better plan and track his/her teaching or learning process. The detailed record will be part of the unique story behind each product made through the platform, which will ultimately be a historical artifact in this crowdsourcing database of fork art history and development.
